Run status

  68 experiments. Best CV AUC: 0.8059 ± 0.0039 (commit fe549aa) — baseline was 0.7445, so +0.0614 (~8.2% relative) improvement.

  Final config:
  - n_estimators=1300, max_depth=9, learning_rate=0.035, min_child_weight=1
  - Numeric features: DepTime, Distance, DepHour, DepMinute
  - Categorical features: Month, DayofMonth, DayOfWeek, UniqueCarrier, Origin, Dest, CarrierHour (interaction)

  Top contributors:
  1. Capacity scaling (n_estimators 30→1300, lr 0.1→0.035): ~0.022
  2. CarrierHour FE + mcw cascade rebisect: ~0.015
  3. max_depth 6→9: ~0.011
  4. DepHour/DepMinute FE: ~0.0088

  Big lesson worth saving: hyperparameters depend on the feature set. The model plateaued at 0.7866 with mcw=10 → I pushed to mcw=80 (gave 0.7882) → adding CarrierHour broke it open and the optimum
  collapsed back to mcw=1 (giving 0.8043+). A "well-tuned" model is well-tuned for its features only.

  Dead ends (confirmed): OriginDest, DayHour, CarrierMonth, log(Distance), ordinal cat versions, MinutesOfDay, max_depth=11, gamma, reg_lambda, subsample/colsample, lossguide, max_bin, dropping DepMinute
  or DepTime.

  The model is at a tight local optimum within the time budget. Awaiting further direction.
